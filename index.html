I am a postdoctoral researcher working with the Event Horizon Telescope (EHT) Collaboration, and a Data Science Fellow at Insight Data Science in Canada.

My undergraduate and graduate degrees are from University of Guanajuato, where I studied Physics and Electrical Engineering.

In addition to research, I have been involved in software development projects. I am a core developer of THEMIS, a model comparison and parameter estimation framework designed for the (EHT). I am also the creator of cwdprophet, a Python library to track crowdfunding campaigns.
